Cock.li offers all its services for free and is one of the only providers that has never required your photo ID, phone number, or any personal information to register or keep your account. We believe this open access to e-mail is critical infrastructure and this conviction drives everything we do.

We encourage anyone with the means to consider making a donation with Monero or Bitcoin. By donating with cryptocurrency you directly help us do what we've always done without compromise.

Cock.li provides two different funds for different purposes, and this allows donators to vote with their money. Do you want your donation to support the people who spend their time maintaining this service, or do you want to donate to our operating expenses? The choice is yours with TEAMSAFU and SERVICESAFU.

TEAMSAFU

By donating to TEAMSAFU you are supporting the people that develop and maintain cock.li. Our team places themselves at extreme risk to operate this service, and those risks have only been understated publicly. TEAMSAFU was added to the service after over a decade of volunteer work, and then shut down after sufficient funding. Now that we are developing our own webmail client, We've re-opened TEAMSAFU to allow users to support our work.

In contrast to the other fund detailed below, by donating to TEAMSAFU you are telling us to spend your donation as we see fit and are telling our team that you appreciate both our work on the service and the risks we take to provide it. To those that recognize our efforts, thank you so much for your support.
